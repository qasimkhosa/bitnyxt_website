StageBook

StageBook

A theatre networking and booking platform created to help actors, actresses, directors, producers, and theatre owners build profiles, discover each other, communicate, and manage bookings.

  • Role-based profiles with biography, city, languages, genres, and availability
  • Messaging, blocking, and moderation workflows for safer communication
  • Booking requests with venue, city, role, rehearsal, amount, and status history
  • Media support for profile photos, avatars, and gallery uploads
This case study was shaped from the uploaded StageBook privacy policy and terms documents, then rewritten into portfolio language for BitNyxt’s website.
