Refund Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026. This policy explains how BitNyxt handles refund requests for digital downloads and hosted software subscriptions.

1. Product categories

BitNyxt sells digital downloads and hosted software subscriptions. Refund handling depends on which category was purchased and whether the issue relates to access, billing, or product mismatch.

2. Digital download purchases

Because digital files are delivered online and can be accessed immediately, change-of-mind refunds are generally not available after delivery has been completed. If a file is inaccessible, corrupted, duplicated by mistake, or materially different from its product description, contact us within 7 days of purchase for review.

3. Subscription plans

Hosted software subscriptions can be canceled before the next renewal date. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically create a refund for the current billing period unless there was a duplicate charge, billing error, failed access delivery, or another issue that BitNyxt confirms in review.

4. Duplicate or accidental charges

If you believe you were charged more than once for the same purchase, contact BitNyxt with the order email and transaction details so we can investigate quickly.

5. Unauthorized purchases

If you believe a transaction was unauthorized, contact us as soon as possible. We may request information needed to investigate the transaction and coordinate with the payment provider where relevant.

6. Non-refundable situations

Refunds are generally not granted for completed digital delivery where no access problem exists, for subscription periods that were already available for use, or for requests that conflict with the stated product description and license terms.

7. How to request a review

Email admin@bitnyxt.com with your name, order email, product name, payment date, and the reason for the request. Adding screenshots or transaction references can speed up review.

8. Review timeline

BitNyxt aims to review refund-related requests within 5 business days, although complex payment investigations may take longer depending on the provider involved.

9. Chargebacks and dispute prevention

We encourage customers to contact BitNyxt before filing a formal payment dispute so we can try to resolve access or billing issues directly.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to admin@bitnyxt.com or through the support page on this website.
