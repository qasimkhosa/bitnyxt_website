Refund Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026. This Refund Policy explains how BitNyxt handles refund requests for digital services such as websites, landing pages, app-facing public pages, and related design or development work.

1. Service Nature

BitNyxt provides digital services and custom work. Because services often begin with planning, design, revisions, and communication time, refunds are not automatic after work has started.

2. Before Work Starts

If payment is made and no work has started yet, you may request a cancellation and refund review by contacting BitNyxt directly.

3. After Work Starts

Once work has started, any refund is evaluated based on the stage of the project, work already completed, time invested, and any delivered files, drafts, or revisions.

4. Custom and Quote-Based Work

For custom or quoted work, refund outcomes may differ depending on the agreed scope, milestone progress, and communication record. Partial refunds may be considered where appropriate.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally not available for completed work, approved deliveries, or delays caused by missing client feedback, content, assets, or decision-making after work has been substantially performed.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund review, contact admin@bitnyxt.com with your name, project details, payment date, and reason for the request.

7. Review Timeline

BitNyxt will review refund requests in good faith and respond within a reasonable time based on the available project and payment information.

8. Contact

Questions about this Refund Policy can be sent to admin@bitnyxt.com or discussed through the official support channels listed on this website.
