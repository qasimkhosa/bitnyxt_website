Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026. This page explains how BitNyxt handles contact inquiries and related communication.

1. Who We Are

BitNyxt provides digital services including websites, landing pages, app-facing pages, and related design and development support.

2. Information We May Receive

When you contact BitNyxt, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, project details, files, screenshots, and communication history.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, discuss projects, prepare proposals, complete requested work, and provide support.

4. Project Materials

Files and project materials shared for review may be stored as reasonably required to complete work and support future revisions.

5. Data Sharing

BitNyxt does not sell personal data. Information may only be shared when needed to deliver services, use normal communication tools, or meet legal obligations.

6. Retention

Inquiry and project communication may be retained for support, recordkeeping, project continuity, and follow-up purposes.

7. Security

Reasonable efforts are taken to protect information, although no online system can guarantee complete security.

8. Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of information you have shared, subject to business and recordkeeping needs.
