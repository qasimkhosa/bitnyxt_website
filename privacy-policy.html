Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026. This policy explains how BitNyxt handles information for website visitors, business inquiries, and the Android apps Muslim Baby Names & Meanings: Islamic Names A-Z and PPSC MCQs.

Publisher: BitNyxt Apps: Muslim Baby Names + PPSC MCQs Public URL: /privacy-policy

What this page covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the BitNyxt website, contact and inquiry channels, and the Android apps Muslim Baby Names & Meanings: Islamic Names A-Z and PPSC MCQs. It is intended to provide a clear public privacy disclosure for users, clients, and Google Play review purposes.

1. Who We Are

BitNyxt provides digital services including websites, landing pages, app-facing pages, and related design and development support. BitNyxt also publishes mobile apps and public support pages connected to those apps.

2. Website Information We May Receive

When you contact BitNyxt through email, phone, WhatsApp, or website contact flows, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, project details, files, screenshots, and communication history.

3. How Website Information Is Used

Website and inquiry information may be used to respond to messages, discuss projects, prepare proposals, complete requested work, and provide support or follow-up communication.

4. Project Materials

Files and project materials shared for review may be stored as reasonably required to complete work, maintain records, and support future revisions or support requests.

5. Android Apps Covered by This Policy

This policy also applies to the following Android apps published by BitNyxt:

  • Muslim Baby Names & Meanings: Islamic Names A-Z with package name com.bitnyxt.islamicnames.
  • PPSC MCQs, an educational MCQ preparation app provided by BitNyxt Inc.

6. App-Specific Privacy Sections

The two mobile apps covered by this page do not use the same data model. The sections below explain their separate privacy behavior in a clearer app-by-app format.

7. Data Sharing

BitNyxt does not sell personal data. Website inquiry information may only be shared when reasonably needed to deliver services, use normal communication tools, work with trusted operational providers, or meet legal obligations.

For the mobile apps, backend, advertising, and consent SDK providers may process information according to their own policies and service terms.

8. Local Device Storage

Both covered apps may store feature-related information locally on the device. Depending on the app, this may include favorites, recent views, local profile state, quiz progress, bookmarks, recent searches, incorrect question history, and app preferences.

If an app is removed, that locally stored information may also be removed by the device or operating system.

9. Retention

Website inquiry and project communication may be retained for support, recordkeeping, project continuity, follow-up, and legal or business administration needs.

Retention may differ by app depending on whether data remains only on the device or is also submitted to backend systems such as feedback, reports, or profile synchronization.

10. Security

Reasonable efforts are taken to protect information, although no online system can guarantee complete security. For the apps, locally stored content and data remain subject to device-level security, while third-party backend, ad, and consent SDK transmissions are handled under those providers' systems and policies.

11. Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of information you have shared with BitNyxt through direct communication, subject to business and recordkeeping needs.

For the mobile apps, users can clear local app data or uninstall the app to remove on-device information. Where applicable, users may also review advertising privacy choices presented in the app.

12. Policy Changes

This page may be updated when BitNyxt changes website practices or when either covered mobile app changes its data handling, advertising behavior, analytics, account systems, purchases, notifications, or backend services.

App Policies

App-specific privacy details

This section gives separate privacy coverage for each published app so the page remains usable for both users and Google Play review.

Muslim Baby Names & Meanings: Islamic Names A-Z

Package: com.bitnyxt.islamicnames

  • The app provides an offline bundled catalog of Muslim baby names and meanings.
  • Favorites and recently viewed names are stored locally on the device.
  • The app does not require user account creation or login.
  • The app uses Google AdMob to display native ads, interstitial ads, and app open ads.
  • The app uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present consent and privacy choices where required.
  • Google advertising and consent services may process device or other identifiers, app activity, and app info or performance information according to their own policies and current disclosures.

PPSC MCQs

Effective date: April 19, 2026

PPSC MCQs is provided by BitNyxt Inc. This section explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the PPSC MCQs mobile application and related services.

PPSC MCQs: Information You May Provide

  • Full name
  • Highest qualification
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Feedback messages and optional contact details
  • MCQ issue or correction reports and optional notes

PPSC MCQs: Information Stored Locally

  • Saved quiz progress
  • Last opened paper or subject
  • Bookmarked or favourite questions
  • Recent subject searches
  • Recent quiz results
  • Incorrect question history
  • Local profile data and local app state related to announcements, ads, and preferences

PPSC MCQs: Automatically Collected Information

  • App version
  • Device platform
  • Basic app interaction data
  • Crash or diagnostic information
  • Network or request metadata where required for service operation

PPSC MCQs: Guest Mode

You may use the app as a Guest without completing a full profile.

  • The app can still be used normally.
  • A local guest profile may be created with the name Guest.
  • Profile fields such as email and mobile number may remain empty.
  • Some information may still be stored locally to support app functionality.

PPSC MCQs: How Information Is Used

  • Provide quiz, progress, bookmark, and revision features
  • Save and restore learning progress
  • Sync profile data when you choose to submit it
  • Receive and review feedback and MCQ issue reports
  • Improve app content and quality
  • Display in-app announcements
  • Operate and troubleshoot the app
  • Serve and manage advertisements where enabled
  • Meet legal, fraud prevention, and security requirements

PPSC MCQs: Backend and Cloud Services

The app uses Supabase to support content delivery and backend storage.

Depending on how you use the app, BitNyxt may store or process profile information you submit, feedback submissions, MCQ report submissions, and app announcement targeting or delivery state where applicable.

If you do not submit profile details, some backend functions may be limited or may rely only on non-identifying app usage data.

PPSC MCQs: Profile Synchronization

If you create or update a profile using real information, your profile details may be synced to backend systems, including your full name, email address, highest qualification, and mobile number.

PPSC MCQs: Feedback and MCQ Reports

If you submit feedback or report an MCQ issue, we may store your message or report details, selected category or reason, optional contact details, question snapshot and answer-related information, and app version or platform information for debugging and review.

PPSC MCQs: Advertising and Privacy Choices

The app uses Google AdMob and related Google Mobile Ads technologies to show ads. Google advertising SDKs may process IP address, device and account identifiers including Advertising ID where applicable, app interaction data, diagnostic information, and fraud prevention or security-related information.

Where required, the app may also use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to gather consent and present privacy choices.

PPSC MCQs: Third Parties, Sharing, and Retention

The app may rely on third-party services including Supabase, Google AdMob, Google User Messaging Platform, and Google Play services. We do not describe the app as selling personal information.

Information may be shared with service providers as needed to operate the app, deliver ads, store submitted feedback or reports, provide backend infrastructure, and maintain security or fraud prevention measures.

Locally stored data may remain on the device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or the app overwrites or removes stored records. Backend-submitted data such as profiles, feedback, and MCQ reports may be retained for operational and support purposes unless removed under internal policy or valid user request.

PPSC MCQs: Children, Security, and International Processing

PPSC MCQs is not directed to children under the age of 13, and BitNyxt does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect information handled through the app. Depending on the services used to operate the app, information may also be processed or stored in countries other than your own, subject to applicable safeguards and provider practices.
