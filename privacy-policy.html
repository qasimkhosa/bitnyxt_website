StageBook Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StageBook collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the StageBook mobile application and related services.

1. Who We Are

StageBook is a theatre-networking and booking platform that helps actors, actresses, directors, producers, and theatre owners create professional profiles, discover each other, communicate, and manage booking requests.

If you have privacy questions, you can contact:

  • Privacy email: admin@bitnyxt.com
  • Support email: admin@bitnyxt.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Role or account type
  • Authentication and session-related information

Profile Information

  • City
  • Biography
  • Languages
  • Genres
  • Experience
  • Availability status
  • Current booking summary
  • Profile photo, avatar, and gallery media

Booking Information

  • Booking dates
  • Venue
  • City
  • Role or performance type
  • Offer amount
  • Rehearsal details
  • Contact person and contact phone
  • Booking status history

Messaging and Communication Information

  • Messages exchanged between users
  • Blocking status between users
  • Support requests
  • Feedback submissions

Technical and Device Information

  • App configuration and environment context needed to operate the service
  • Local cached session and profile state on device
  • Basic usage context associated with app screens or support requests

3. How We Use Information

We use personal and operational information to:

  • create and manage user accounts
  • authenticate users and maintain sessions
  • allow users to build and edit profiles
  • enable professional discovery inside the app
  • send, receive, and manage booking requests
  • send, receive, and manage messages
  • support moderation, blocking, verification, and suspension workflows
  • store and display profile media
  • respond to support, privacy, and feedback requests
  • improve service reliability, security, and product quality

4. Where Data Is Stored

StageBook uses a hosted backend and related storage services to operate the app.

This may include:

  • authentication services
  • database storage for profiles, bookings, messages, and feedback
  • media storage for profile photos, avatars, and gallery uploads

The app may also cache limited information locally on your device, such as session and persisted app state, to support sign-in continuity and app usability across launches.

5. Profile Visibility and In-App Sharing

StageBook is designed as a professional discovery platform. Information you add to your profile may be visible to other users in the app, subject to product rules and role-based access.

Examples include:

  • name
  • role
  • city
  • profile biography
  • availability status
  • profile images

Certain private or operational information, such as booking details, messages, email addresses, phone numbers, or moderation-related information, is not intended to be publicly visible to all users and may be restricted to:

  • the account owner
  • participants in a booking or conversation
  • authorized administrative or support workflows

6. Messages, Bookings, and Blocking

When you use StageBook messaging and booking features:

  • messages may be stored so the relevant users can access conversation history
  • bookings may be stored so participants can review request details and status
  • user blocking may restrict further messaging while preserving conversation history
  • moderation controls may be applied to support safety and platform integrity

7. Media Uploads

If you upload a profile image, avatar, or gallery photo:

  • the file may be stored with StageBook’s hosted media/storage provider
  • the upload may be linked to your account profile
  • the media may be displayed in-app according to the product’s profile visibility rules

8. Feedback and Support

If you submit feedback or contact support, StageBook may store:

  • your message
  • category or support context
  • optional contact details you provide
  • related app context needed to investigate your request

We use this information to improve the app, investigate problems, and communicate with you if follow-up is needed.

9. Legal Basis and Platform Operation

We process information as needed to:

  • provide the app and its core features
  • operate and secure the platform
  • respond to user requests
  • prevent abuse and misuse
  • maintain service integrity
  • comply with legal obligations

10. Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to:

  • operate the service
  • maintain security and auditability
  • resolve disputes
  • enforce platform rules
  • comply with legal obligations

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

11. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request:

  • access to your personal data
  • correction of inaccurate data
  • deletion of personal data
  • help with account or privacy issues

These requests may be subject to legal, operational, fraud-prevention, security, or record-keeping requirements.

12. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect account and service data, including the use of hosted backend infrastructure, authentication controls, and administrative restrictions. However, no service can guarantee absolute security.

13. Third-Party Services

StageBook may rely on third-party providers for services such as:

  • authentication
  • hosting
  • database storage
  • file storage
  • email or support workflows

Those providers may process information as part of operating the app.

14. Children

StageBook is intended for professional and adult use in the theatre industry. It is not designed for children.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published through our public legal pages and may also be referenced inside the app.

16. Contact

For privacy or support questions:

  • Privacy email: admin@bitnyxt.com
  • Support email: admin@bitnyxt.com
