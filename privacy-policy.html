Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026. This policy explains how BitNyxt handles information from website visitors, product buyers, support requests, and users of BitNyxt hosted software.

Controller: BitNyxt Scope: Website, purchases, support Public URL: /privacy-policy

What this page covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the BitNyxt website, contact channels, downloadable digital products, and hosted software services offered through this domain. It does not describe unrelated Android apps or third-party businesses.

1. Information we may receive

BitNyxt may receive your name, email address, billing email, order details, support messages, usage details for hosted products, and other information you choose to share when contacting us or purchasing a product.

2. Website inquiries

If you email us or use support contact methods, we may receive your contact details, message contents, attachments, and communication history so we can respond and keep support records.

3. Purchases and billing

When a purchase is processed through a payment provider or merchant-of-record, that provider may collect billing, tax, payment, fraud-prevention, and transaction details under its own privacy terms. BitNyxt may receive order status, customer email, product purchased, country, and other fulfillment-related information required to deliver the product and support the order.

4. Hosted product usage

For subscription software, BitNyxt may process account details, login events, workspace activity, support actions, and basic operational logs needed to provide access, secure the service, and troubleshoot problems.

5. How information is used

Information may be used to deliver digital products, provide hosted access, process support requests, send purchase-related emails, prevent abuse, improve product stability, and comply with legal or tax obligations.

6. Data sharing

BitNyxt does not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with service providers that help us operate the website, process payments, deliver digital products, host software, communicate with customers, or meet legal obligations.

7. Cookies and analytics

The website or hosted products may use essential cookies or basic analytics needed for security, performance, or product operation. If optional analytics are used, they should remain proportionate to the needs of a software business.

8. Retention

We may retain order records, support history, and operational logs for as long as reasonably required for fulfillment, product support, fraud prevention, legal compliance, recordkeeping, and dispute resolution.

9. International processing

BitNyxt is an online business and may use service providers in different countries. Information may therefore be processed outside your country, subject to the safeguards and practices of those providers.

10. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, but we aim to limit access and use reputable infrastructure providers.

11. Your choices

You may contact BitNyxt to request updates, corrections, or deletion of information you shared directly, subject to legal, tax, fraud-prevention, and recordkeeping requirements.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to admin@bitnyxt.com or through the support page on this domain.
