2. Information We May Receive

When you contact BitNyxt through email, phone, WhatsApp, or the website contact form, we may receive information such as:

BitNyxt does not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to deliver the requested service, use basic communication tools, hosting, file transfer, or comply with legal obligations.

Inquiry and project communication may be retained for business continuity, project follow-up, revisions, recordkeeping, and support purposes for as long as reasonably needed.

Reasonable efforts are taken to protect contact and project information. However, no communication channel or online storage method can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of personal information you have shared, subject to project, legal, or business recordkeeping needs.
